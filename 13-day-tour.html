The Grand Tapestry: A 13-Day Expedition

For the discerning traveler who seeks a truly comprehensive understanding of Morocco, this is our masterpiece. The 13-day grand expedition is a sweeping narrative that weaves together every thread of the Moroccan identity: its imperial cities, remote mountains, vast deserts, and windswept coastlines.

Threads of the Journey

This itinerary is a symphony of experiences. Below are the key movements of your two-week immersion into the soul of Morocco.

  • The Imperial Quartet: You will explore the distinct character of all four Imperial Cities. From the stately avenues of Rabat and the grand gates of Meknes to the ancient intellectualism of Fes and the vibrant pulse of Marrakech.
  • The Blue Dream of Chefchaouen: Discover the tranquil, blue-washed streets of Chefchaouen, a city nestled in the Rif Mountains that offers a serene counterpoint to the imperial capitals.
  • Roman Echoes & Holy Towns: Journey back in time at the impressive Roman ruins of Volubilis before exploring the sacred town of Moulay Idriss.
  • The Saharan Immersion: The journey includes a multi-day exploration of the desert, culminating in a camel trek into the Erg Chebbi dunes for a night spent under a canopy of innumerable stars.
  • Atlas Crossings & Gorges: You will traverse both the Middle and High Atlas mountain ranges, discovering the cedar forests of Azrou and the breathtaking, river-carved canyons of the Todra and Dades Gorges.
  • The Atlantic Breeze: The grand tour concludes with a journey to the coast, to the charming, fortified port town of Essaouira, known for its whitewashed walls, fresh seafood, and bohemian spirit.

A Journey Without Compromise

This expedition is for those who do not wish to choose, but to experience it all. It is a deep, unhurried, and luxurious immersion into one of the world's most captivating countries. Every day brings a new landscape, a new story, a new shade of Morocco.
